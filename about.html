Who We Are

Fabricity was founded in 2025 by Tlhongbotho Mercy Dichaba with a passion for expressive fashion and a vision to make personal style accessible to everyone.

As a Bsc in Computer Systems Engineering student, I bring a unique perspective to fashion - blending technical precision with creative expression. My personal style is deeply influenced by my mood and the music I'm listening to at any given moment, and this philosophy is at the heart of Fabricity.

We believe fashion should be a form of self-expression without boundaries, where your outfit reflects who you are in that moment. Whether you're feeling bold, relaxed, or somewhere in between, we've got pieces that will help you express it.

Tlhongbotho Mercy Dichaba
